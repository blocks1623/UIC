Our Leader

Prophet Jay Uriel

"Before I formed you in the womb I knew you, before you were born I set you apart; I appointed you as a prophet to the nations." — Jeremiah 1:5

Prophet Jay Uriel is a consecrated vessel called by God — a prophetic voice and teacher of the Word raised up for this generation. With a burning passion for truth and a heart surrendered to the Holy Spirit, he carries a mantle of revelation that transforms lives.

Through the ministry of United in Christ, Prophet Jay Uriel brings the uncompromised Word of God to all nations — teaching, preaching, and releasing prophetic revelation that draws people into deeper encounters with the living God.
